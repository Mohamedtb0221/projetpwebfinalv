Who we are

We were Created in 1985 at USTHB, Micro Club is the first scientific club university in Algeria. As a non-profit, it works to popularize tion and introduction to computers, in particular through the organization targeted training and events.

OUR GOAL

Micro Club aims not only to arm the students with a background of technical skills information, through the promotion of information matics and the various IT fields, but also encourages familiarization and integration to the professional world by encouraging the development of relations between our student community and the world of take-up. In particular, we encourage members assets and members of the club to improve the scientific production, within the campus in particular, but also on all National territory.

Some of club history


Micro Club is the first founded scientific club in Algeria, back in March 5th, 1985. We focuse on Computer Science and IT field as we work to popularize among the student community
